## 🎨 Design System

**Color Palette**:
- Primary Blue: #0074b3 (main brand color)
- Teal: #319795 (accent)
- Green: #38b2ac (success/health)
- Purple: #7c3aed (premium features)
- Gray: Full scale for text/backgrounds

**Typography**:
- Font: Inter (professional, modern, readable)
- Headers: Bold, 40-60px
- Body: Regular, 16-20px

**Components**:
- Gradient backgrounds for visual interest
- Rounded corners (xl = 12px, 2xl = 16px)
- Shadow elevations for depth
- Icons and emojis for visual cues
- Cards with hover effects
